Post-Purchase Steps

What should happen after someone buys a blueprint

This page is the buyer-facing version of the delivery workflow. It can be used as a thank-you or post-purchase instructions page once the Shopify product is live.

Back to Blueprint Overview View Sample Reports

Delivery Timeline

Target: delivered within 1 business day

Step 1

Order confirmation

The order should include the business name, business type, bottleneck, budget, and plan delivery email so the report can be personalized properly.

Step 2

Intake review

The intake is reviewed to make sure there is enough detail to build a useful blueprint instead of sending a generic result.

Step 3

Blueprint delivery

The finished blueprint should be delivered to the plan email address within 1 business day, assuming no follow-up is needed.

Included

Budget and sequencing

What to buy now, what to delay, and how to avoid spending too early on the wrong layer.

First workflows and rollout

Upgrade path

If The Intake Is Incomplete

What to do when follow-up is needed

Need Clarification

Ask one clear follow-up question, not ten

If the business type, bottleneck, or delivery email is missing, follow up quickly and keep the questions narrow so the order does not stall.

Best Practice

Keep the delivery promise realistic

Use the "within 1 business day" timeline only when the intake is complete enough to produce a useful report without a long back-and-forth.

Trust Builder

Point buyers to sample reports

Use the report previews to show exactly what the blueprint looks like before and after purchase so the buyer knows what to expect.
