Meeting Workflow

Best AI stack for meeting notes and follow-up.

Meeting AI only becomes valuable when the notes land somewhere useful. The real win is not transcription alone. It is turning calls into action items, CRM updates, and cleaner follow-up.

When this stack matters most

  • Client or sales meetings are frequent enough that recap work keeps slipping
  • Leads or customer tasks die after the call because nobody owns the follow-up
  • The team needs searchable meeting memory, not scattered notes across inboxes and docs

Typical starter stack

  • Fathom, Fireflies, Otter, or Zoom AI Companion for capture and recap
  • Notion AI, Slack, or ClickUp for routing and searchable follow-up
  • HubSpot or Pipedrive if notes need to land against lead and customer records
  • Zapier if summaries need to move between tools automatically

Best first workflow

Meeting transcript -> AI summary -> action items -> CRM or task update -> client recap. Teams that make that flow consistent usually stop losing work after the meeting ends.
