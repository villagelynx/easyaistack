AI Agents

What an AI agent actually means for a small business.

An AI agent is not just a chatbot that writes text back to you. It is an AI system that can take a goal, use business context, look things up in connected systems, and carry part of the task forward before handing off to a person when needed.

Where agents usually help first

  • Support: answer questions, route tickets, and hand off harder issues with context
  • Sales: summarize inquiries, update the CRM, and prep next-step drafts
  • Finance and ops: surface insights, complete repetitive steps, and reduce manual cleanup

What to look for

  • Access to the business context, not just a general prompt box
  • Clear handoff rules so people stay in control
  • One defined workflow first, instead of trying to automate the whole company at once

Common small-business agent examples

Intercom for support agents, HubSpot and Salesforce Agentforce for go-to-market workflows, Amazon Q Business for internal knowledge, Aircall for call handling, and Intuit Intelligence for finance-side work are all examples of this move toward more agent-like tools.
