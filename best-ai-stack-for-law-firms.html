Legal AI Stack

Best AI stack for law firms.

Law firms usually benefit most from AI when it strengthens research, intake summaries, internal drafting, and document review workflows without pushing too quickly into sensitive client-facing automation.

Run the Planner Browse Research-Focused Tools

Start with lower-risk internal workflows

Firms usually see the fastest safe wins in research support, internal summaries, intake cleanup, and meeting recap. Those uses create leverage without depending on unchecked client-facing automation.

Typical legal starter stack

  • Claude for longer-form drafting, document summaries, and policy-heavy internal work
  • Perplexity for faster cited research and source-backed prep
  • Notion AI or Coda for internal notes, matter templates, and searchable process docs
  • Otter or Fathom when meetings and intake calls generate cleanup work

What to watch closely

Legal teams should keep human review, source checking, and documented process rules close to the workflow. The right AI stack is usually the one that removes admin drag while still respecting review standards and risk tolerance.
