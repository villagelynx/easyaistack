Lead Follow-Up Stack

Build an AI lead follow-up stack without overbuying software.

The best lead follow-up stack is usually not five new tools. It is one assistant layer, one place to track the lead, and one workflow that moves faster from inquiry to response.

Run the Planner See the $29 Plan

What this stack needs to do

  • Capture the inquiry cleanly from form, email, or chat
  • Summarize or qualify the lead fast enough for an owner or team member to act
  • Route the lead into a CRM, task list, or booking workflow without manual retyping

Typical starter stack

  • ChatGPT or Claude for summaries, reply drafts, and qualification notes
  • Pipedrive or HubSpot for lead tracking and next-step ownership
  • Zapier if the business needs form-to-CRM or form-to-task routing
  • Calendly if the next step is fast scheduling instead of long back-and-forth email

Best first workflow

New inquiry -> AI summary -> CRM update -> owner follow-up or booking link. If that single flow becomes reliable, the business usually feels the benefit immediately in speed, consistency, and reduced admin cleanup.
