Agency AI Stack

Best AI stack for agencies.

The best agency stack usually combines a strong assistant, a meeting layer, a delivery ops layer, and one content or automation layer tied to the agency's main offer.

Agencies usually need cross-tool consistency

Agencies create value in the handoff between sales, meetings, strategy, production, and client delivery. That makes stack quality more about process continuity than about any one AI writing tool.

Typical agency growth stack

  • ChatGPT or Claude for strategy, copy, and internal drafts
  • Notion AI or Airtable for operating docs and delivery systems
  • ClickUp or Asana for recurring client work
  • Fathom, Fireflies, or Otter for client-call cleanup
  • Canva, Descript, or Riverside if content production is central

Best first workflow

Client meeting -> AI recap -> task creation -> client follow-up. Agencies that stabilize that flow usually feel the gains across delivery, internal coordination, and account management almost immediately.
